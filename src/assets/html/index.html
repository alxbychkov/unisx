UniSX decentralised
derivatives

New synthetic, collateral-backed tokens whose value changes depending on exhange quotes. Synthetics are created by depositing collateral into a smart contract and minting tokens backed by that collateral.

How
it works

Mint

Mint UniSX tokens and get sponsor revards. You can trade your tokens or deposit them to DEX liquidity pools.

Stake

Stake UniSX tokens to smart contract and earn additional GT

Pool

Deposit UniSX synthetic tokens in the corresponding Uniswap pool to earn OPIUM & additional UniSX liquidity provider rewards.

Trade

You can trade UniSX synthetic tokens on DEX to yarn the difference between minting cost and the synthetics spot price as profit.
